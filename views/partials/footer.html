Insufficient funds collected

Once registry closes, if the total donated amount is insufficient to buy the pre-specified gift, The Leaving Gift will either a) choose an appropriate similar gift of lesser value or b) use donations to purchase vouchers from a well-established internet retailer. If total donated amount exceeds the value of the gift specified, The Leaving Gift will either a) buy vouchers with the additional money or b) buy an upgraded version of the specified gift. The Leaving Gift will only operate its services when total donations on each gift registry page equal, or exceed, 5 GBP by the registry closing date; total donations less than 5 GBP will be returned to the donators.
